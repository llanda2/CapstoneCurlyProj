Helpful Tips & Tools

{% comment %} Tools Section {% endcomment %}

Wide-Tooth Comb

Perfect for gently detangling curly and wavy hair without breaking up natural curl patterns or causing breakage. Use on wet hair with conditioner to minimize tugging and knots.

Satin or Silk Pillowcase

Reduces friction to control frizz and prevent breakage. Preserves curls overnight for fresher morning hair.

Bonnet

A satin or silk bonnet locks in moisture and reduces frizz while you sleep. Keeps hair manageable and defined.

Scrunchies

Gentle on curls and perfect for putting hair up without causing dents or disrupting curl formation.

Diffuser Attachment

Essential for evenly drying curly hair without frizz. Maintains natural curl patterns while adding volume and definition.

Microfiber Hair Towel

Reduces friction and prevents breakage and frizz. Absorbs water without over-drying hair, keeping curls hydrated and bouncy.

Old T-Shirt

A budget-friendly alternative to towels for drying hair. Great for "plopping," enhancing curl definition while hair dries.

Spray Bottle

Revives second- or third-day curls by reactivating product and bringing bounce back to lifeless waves or curls.
