Helpful Tips & FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I wash my curly hair? ▶
It depends on your hair type and personal preference, but generally 1-2 times a week is enough to maintain moisture while keeping your scalp clean.
What is "plopping"? ▶
Plopping is a method of using a cotton T-shirt or microfiber towel to enhance curl formation while your hair dries, reducing frizz and promoting definition.
How can I refresh my curls between wash days? ▶
Use a spray bottle with water or a curl refresher spray to rehydrate and reactivate your styling products. Scrunch gently to bring back your curl shape.
Should I brush my curls? ▶
Avoid brushing dry curls to prevent breakage and frizz. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle when your hair is wet and conditioned.

Helpful Tools

Wide-Tooth Comb ▶
Gently detangle curly and wavy hair without breaking curl patterns. Best on wet hair with conditioner to prevent knots.
Satin or Silk Pillowcase ▶
Reduces friction while you sleep, preventing frizz and breakage. Preserves curl shape for longer.
Bonnet ▶
Protects curls overnight, locking in moisture and reducing frizz. Helps curls stay defined and manageable.
Scrunchies ▶
Gentle on curls and avoids dents or breakage caused by regular hair ties.
Diffuser Attachment ▶
Distributes heat evenly while drying curls, minimizing frizz and maintaining curl shape.
Microfiber Hair Towel ▶
Reduces frizz and breakage by absorbing water gently. Keeps curls hydrated and bouncy.
Old T-Shirt ▶
A cost-effective way to dry hair gently, enhancing curl definition and minimizing frizz.
Spray Bottle ▶
Refresh second- or third-day curls by rehydrating and reactivating styling products.
